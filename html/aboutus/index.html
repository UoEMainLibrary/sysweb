Service Definition

User Requirements
Systems Support provides helpdesk, a fault logging and resolution service, to all library staff. Through helpdesk Systems Support deals with faults to existing computer equipment and software provided to library staff for carrying out work tasks by the University Library.

Helpdesk support is available Monday-Friday 8am - 5pm during term. Requests for helpdesk support should be sent to EUL.Systems.Support@ed.ac.uk. All helpdesk calls are logged and dealt with according to a published level of priority. Helpdesk calls which affect public services will be given top priority.

Systems Development also provides assistance and advice to library staff on new developments in computer software and hardware and their implementation. Requests for advice or for assistance with development work should be sent to Library.development@ed.ac.uk. Priority is allocated in the Divisional workplan and according to perceived user need. Development work is not a fault but a request for an enhancement to an existing service or for the establishment of a new service which will be supported by Systems Support.

Systems Support co-ordinates the installation, support and maintenance of all computer hardware and software purchased by Edinburgh University Library.

Requests for new computer equipment and software should be sent to EUL.Systems.Support@ed.ac.uk and should be supported by the completion of the appropriate equipment request form available from the General Office.
