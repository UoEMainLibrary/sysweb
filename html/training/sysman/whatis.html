Systems Manual

What is the Systems Manual?

The Systems Manual has been created to help educate library staff on the main issues associated with IT in the Library.

The manual should be consulted before contacting the Systems Helpdesk, this will eliminate any possible solutions before further investigation is carried out.

The manual will be updated on a regular basis to include the latest software and hardware developments on the Common Desktop. Your Site Representative will be responsible for the update of your local Systems Manual and will act as a central point for any enquiries.

Should you have any request for additions to the Systems Manual, please email the following address: manual@srv4.lib.ed.ac.uk
